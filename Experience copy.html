Experience

Things I've done in my life - including work experience

I’ve had the chance to work as an assistant at a tax attorney’s law office, where I helped with organizing documents, scheduling, and communicating with clients. It taught me to pay attention to details and handle sensitive information responsibly. I’ve also worked as a teacher for a small group of kids, teaching science and history. It was a lot of fun creating lessons and making the subjects interesting while helping the kids learn and grow.

I spent three months living in Romania, working in an orphanage with highly disabled children. It was a life-changing experience where I learned so much about patience, compassion, and adaptability. Every day was about building connections, providing care, and finding small ways to bring joy and comfort to the kids. It wasn’t always easy, but it taught me the value of kindness and how meaningful even the smallest efforts can be in someone’s life.

I served a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in San Diego and Colorado Springs. In San Diego, I worked at the Mormon Battalion Historic Site, sharing the stories and history of early pioneers while engaging with visitors from around the world. In Colorado Springs, I focused on service and community outreach, working closely with individuals and families to offer support and encouragement.
